   Expected Value: ~22,000 coins average

   Reward: 50,000-200,000 coins or legendary item
   Mimic Chance: 20%
   Expected Value: ~100,000 coins average
   Balance: Highest risk, highest reward
   Use case: Endgame gambling for huge gains
   ROI: 100% profit on average (if not mimic)
   Risk: 1 in 5 chance to lose everything (mimic)

   Effect: Prevents next chest from being a mimic
   Use case: Before opening expensive chests
   Strategy: Always use before Legendary chests!
   Value: Insurance policy (worth it for Epic+)

- Mimics eat your chest = total loss
- Higher tier = higher mimic chance
- Risk vs reward: bigger chests = bigger danger
- Protection is insurance for big opens

CHEST STRATEGY:
Beginner: Common chests only (safe)
Mid-game: Rare chests without protection
Advanced: Epic chests WITH protection
Expert: Legendary WITH protection (expected +47.5k profit)

BALANCE NOTES:
- Expected value slightly positive (encourages buying)
- Mimic chance scales with tier (balances big rewards)
- Protection item creates strategic depth
- Good coin sink for wealthy players
