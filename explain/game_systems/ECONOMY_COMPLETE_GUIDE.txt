üí≥ LOAN SYSTEM
===========================================

WHAT ARE LOANS?
Borrow coins now, pay back with interest
over time through EMI (installments).

COMMAND: /takeloan <amount>

HOW IT WORKS:
1. Request loan amount
2. System calculates:
   - Interest rate (10%)
   - Total repayment amount
   - EMI (weekly payment)
   - Number of payments
3. Approve and get coins instantly
4. Pay back with /payloan

LOAN TERMS:
Loan Amount: 1,000 - 1,000,000 coins
Interest Rate: 10% total
Payment Period: 4 weeks (4 EMIs)
EMI: Total amount √∑ 4

EXAMPLES:

3. Can't overpay (auto-refunds excess)
